Sharing Inspiration gems-

Blogs-

  1. Emma Collin’s blog-
    http://www.emmacollinscoaching.com/blog/
    Emma Collins speaks about loving and living fully. She draws from her own life experiences of joys and grief she has experienced and shares how she lives a little more wholeheartedly. I am hugely inspired by her.
  2. Miki kashtan’s blog- The Fearless heart-
    http://thefearlessheart.org/blog/
    Miki Kashtan talks about Non Violent Communication & transforming systems in communities globally to support everyone’s needs. I find hope, clarity and precision in her words.
  3. Lyn Georgy’s blog- Psychchronicles-
    https://psychchronicles.wordpress.com/
    Lyn Georgy, a dear friend, writes with a lot of integrity about mental health, an area which needs to become a part of mainstream conversation globally. I find a lot of inspiration in the care she takes in choosing her words, and in the thoroughness of research in her articles.
